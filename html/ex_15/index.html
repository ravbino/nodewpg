Tips for a success oral presentation


If you want to achieve a successful presentation, you have to keep in mind some recommendations and make a previous task. Some of those recommendations have been collected from people with a great experience in this form of presentation. Here are some tips:

- Before

o Make outlines with main information and the order of presentation
o Keep in your mind all information you need
 Names, dates, places, etc.
o Try recording yourself and use repeating and shadowing

- During

o Adjust your volume according to the number of attendees
o Pace with the words
o Expression, make an exciting presentation
o Body language

- Don’t

• Have your hands in your pockets
• Fidget
• Sway from side to side


- Do

• Stand up straight
• Face your audience
• Use your hands to help you present
• Smile
o Eye Contact
 Don’t read
 Memorize
 Maintain eye contact with each of your audience member
o Speak fluently

- After

o Don’t forget to thank you
o Make a review in your mind
o With someone you trust, ask for feed back
